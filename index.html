Eperienced Cardiothoracic Surgery 1st Assist Providers Needed at Established Heart & Vascular Institutes  

Sign-on Bonus, Student Loan Allowence, and Relocation Assistance Offered!

Carle Health is seeking additional CT 1st Assist Advanced Practice Providers to join our established Heart & Vascular Institute at Carle Foundation Hospital in Urbana, IL and Carle BroMenn Medical Center in Norman, IL.



Position Details Include:
  • At least 2 years of experience working as an Advanced Practice Provider in Cardiothoracic Surgery is required - including surgical first-assisting. Endoscopic Vein Harvest (EVH), and perioperative patient care. APPs that have surgical experience in another specialty may be
  • Established practices with BC Cardiac Surgeons and experienced Advanced Practice Providers.
  • Opportunity to develop sub practice based on clinical interests.
  • Comprehensive practice that includes surgery, clinic, and hospital rounding, allowing 1st Assist APP to practice full scope.
  • In addition to competive compensation, we offer an excellent benefits package that includes: sign on bonus, student loan allowence, health dental/life insurance, 403-B plan with employer match, LTD, relocation allowence, CME allowence, and paid malpractice insurance with 100% tail insurance covered.

Based in Urbana, IL, The Carle Foundation is a vertically integrated system with more than 9,500 employees in its five hospitals, multi-specialty physician groups, health plan and associated healthcare businesses including the Carle Illinois College of Medicine, the world's first engineering-based medical school. Carle is proud to be named a Great Place to Work. Carle Foundation Hospital and Carle BroMenn Medical Center hold Magnet designation, the nation's highest honor for nursing care and Carle Foundation Hospital (CFH) ranks as one of America's 50 Best Hospitals by Healthgrades.
 


About Our Communities 


Champaign-Urbana and Bloomington-Normal are both mid-size, family-friendly communities centrally located to Chicago, St. Louis and Indianapolis. Home to nationally-ranked institutions such as the Univesity of Illinois and Illinois State University, both towns offer an abundance of dining, arts, sports, and entertainment options and have easy access to regional and international airports.
